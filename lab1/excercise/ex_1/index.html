About Our Department

The Computer Science Department is dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. We provide students with strong foundations in programming, algorithms, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity.

Our faculty members conduct cutting-edge research in various fields including machine learning, data science, distributed systems, and software engineering. We aim to prepare students for global careers in technology and research.

We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
